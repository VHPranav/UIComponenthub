## Overview

The **Team Section** is a high-engagement layout component designed to showcase people with personality. Instead of a standard grid, it uses overlapping cards with randomized rotations and vibrant background colors to create a "stacked photo" aesthetic. It features smooth transitions between member sets and interactive "Hi" tooltips that respond to mouse movement.

## Features

- **Overlapping Card Layout**: Uses negative margins and calculated Z-indices for a depth-rich, "casual" look.
- **Set-Based Navigation**: High-performance transition between sets of team members using `AnimatePresence`.
- **Interactive Tooltips**: Animated labels that show member names and roles on hover.
- **Dynamic Rotations**: Each card has a slight rotation offset to mimic physical photos spread on a table.
- **Full Responsiveness**: Automatically hides less critical team members on smaller screens to maintain the layout's integrity.

## How it works

1. **Stacking**: Cards are positioned in a flex container with negative horizontal margins (`-ml-12`). The Z-index is calculated based on the index (`total - index`) so they stack correctly from left to right.
2. **Set Transitions**: The component chunks a large array of members into smaller sets (default is 6). A timer or dot-navigation triggers a state change that swaps these sets with a horizontal slide animation.
3. **Hover Physics**: When a card is hovered, it scales up (`scale-110`), resets its rotation to `0deg`, and its Z-index is boosted to `100` to bring it to the absolute front of the stack.
4. **Motion**: Headings and subtext use staggered `motion.div` entrances to guide the user's eye from the top down to the interactive team cards.
